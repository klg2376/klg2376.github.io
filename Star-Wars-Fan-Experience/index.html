Welcome

The Star Wars Universe, for most people, exists within the realms of the Original, Prequel, and Sequel Trilogies and the new Anthology films that Disney and Lucasfilm Ltd. are producing and releasing over the next several years. However, the Universe goes far beyond the reach that the films have created. Some of the elements that have been created since the initial release of Episode IV: A New Hope inlude:

  • A vast array of TV series, books, comics, and games that make up both the Canon and Expanded Universes,
  • The Star Wars Celebration annual convention,
  • And works created by fans to celebrate and experience the series on a deeper and more meaningfull level.

On this site, you will be able to explore each of these elements of the Star Wars Universe, as well as learn about the 40 years of history behind one of cinema's most iconic and well known Science Fiction series.

Enjoy your stay in a galaxy far, far away and may the Force be with you.
