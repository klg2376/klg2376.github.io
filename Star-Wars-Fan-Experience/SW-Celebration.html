Star Wars Celebration 2017

Star Wars Celebration is the annual Star Wars convention for fans of the series. The most recent rendition of the convention was the 2016 convention in London, England. This convention featured not only merchandise, but also a new trailer for the film Rogue One, information for the third season of Star Wars Rebels, and appearances by the members of the casts of Rogue One and The Force Awakens. Significant appearances include Mark Hamill (Luke Skywalker), Carrie Fischer (Leia Organa), and Gwendoline Christie (Captain Phasma).

The 2017 convention is the 12th rendition of the convention and the 7th within the United States. It will take place April 13-16 in Orlando Florida. Tickets are now available and can be purchased here.
